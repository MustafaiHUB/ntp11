The National Technology Parade

The University of Jordan, and other leading Jordanian Universities and in cooperation with Queen Rania Center for Entrepreneurship (QRCE) are organizing the eighth Annual Technology Parade in April 2015. The Technology Parade comes in response to the global drive towards capitalizing on the innovative potential of students to provide technological solutions to challenges facing businesses, governments, civil society, and local communities in the different areas of the world.
