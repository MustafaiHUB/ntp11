The 11th National Technology Parade

The University of Jordan, and other leading Jordanian Universities are organizing the eleventh National Technology Parade (NTP) in May 2024. This event is a direct response to the worldwide push to harness students' innovative capabilities in offering technological answers to the various challenges encountered by businesses, governments, civil society, and local communities across different regions globally.
